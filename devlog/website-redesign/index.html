Website Redesign

May 2013

It’s been a long time since Bytesize Adventures had a face lift. The previous aesthetic was heavily tied to Coffee Cellar, having been built at roughly the same time, with the two sharing similar colours and fonts.

I’ve been feeling a desire to move the blog away from a sole focus on iOS development. My interests, and posts, have naturally taken me in a few different directions.

Recent web trends have placed content in an even more important role than ever. Social networking sites such as Twitter have encouraged people to consume content at a faster rate, and thus, in smaller, more manageable chunks.

My new design is all about putting the content first. I’ve dropped the decoration and focussed on readability, delivering the content in a distraction free environment.

Its about my converging interests.

I hope you like the new site and, more importantly, I hope you enjoy the content.
