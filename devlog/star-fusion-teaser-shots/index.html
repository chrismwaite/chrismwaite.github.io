Star Fusion Teaser Shots

July 2009

The release date for my first iPhone game is drawing ever closer. All of the mechanics are in place and i’m currently tweaking the graphics, sounds, and game difficulty. Within the next day or so I expect to move onto the final round of bug testing and code refactoring.

I should mention that I have a lot of ideas for extending the game further but I feel it’s important to draw a line under the feature set to ensure I release it in a reasonable time frame. I’ll hopefully release the additional features as updates to the game once it’s live.

One final important announcement, i’ve settled on the final name for the game. What was Sunlight is now STAR FUSION which should be available to buy from the iPhone Application Store sometime in July. I’ll leave you with a couple of teaser shots to wet your appetite.
