Star Fusion approved by Apple

July 2009

Star Fusion has just been approved by Apple and is available to buy now from the iPhone App Store. Its great to finally see my first game available for sale and I can’t wait to see the reaction from people who play it. I’ll write a more in depth post over the coming days. I’ll also share with you some of the statistics, as well as my experiences, as I attempt to promote the game.
