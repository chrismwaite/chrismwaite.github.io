Coffee Cellar 2.0 is out now

November 2012

After many months of hard work, Coffee Cellar 2.0 is at last out in the wild.

If you’re an existing Coffee Cellar use then you’ll get the update for free. It should appear in your updates very soon.

The rest of you can grab the App here. You know, if you like coffee and cool Apps that is.

If you’re not yet convinced, check out the official website. We also have Twitter and Facebook.
